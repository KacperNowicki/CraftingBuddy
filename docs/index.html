The flow

Open CraftingBuddy.
Pick your WoW folder and install the addon.
Scan in WoW.
Run Auctionator scan, open CPE from the minimap, press Scan all + variants, then /reload.
Generate report.
The app detects your realm and uses Undermine API or Goblin Exchange market data.
Craft from the top down.
Use market confidence and shopping-list buttons to stay out of low-movement traps.

Why it exists

Personal profit

CraftSim already knows your character stats, reagent quality breakpoints, multicraft, and concentration costs. CraftingBuddy keeps using that instead of guessing.

Market sanity

A huge margin is useless if nobody buys the item. The report surfaces daily movement, stock, and confidence labels.

Exact mats

Mixed-quality reagent paths are preserved all the way into the Auctionator shopping-list payload.
